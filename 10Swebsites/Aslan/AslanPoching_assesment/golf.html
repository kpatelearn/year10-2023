Welcome to Golf !

Golf is a club-and-ball sport in which players try to use as few strokes as possible to smash balls into a sequence of holes on a course. Here at Aslandia we offer 4 different area for you to play. with every ticket you are given a bucket of 25 balls and a club, if you have brought your own gear please let the staff at the golf counter and they will give you a 5 dollar refund. Courses are open 24/7 and You can find a trainer to work on your swing with you at all times.
