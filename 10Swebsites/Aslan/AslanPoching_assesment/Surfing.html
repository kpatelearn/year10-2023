Welcome to surfing!

Surfing is a surface water sport that uses a board to ride the waves. Here at aslandia we offer classes to all level surfers from beginners all the way to advanced classes. they start at 10:00am sharp and run all the way upto sunset, so we can find a time that suits you. All equipment is free to hire and feel free to bring your own equipment. Our classes are sure to give you confidence for your next surf trip!
