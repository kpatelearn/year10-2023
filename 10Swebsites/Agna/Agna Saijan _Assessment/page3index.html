About us

Our shop started in the 1920s. Recipes made nearly a century ago have been passed down in our shop. Within the first 20 years in Auckland, "Cakes and Bakes" became a huge success. People buy our cakes for weddings, birthdays, parties, and other events. It is also a tourist food destination. Many people who have visited Auckland have visited Cakes and Bakes. We make our cakes with the best ingredients. We have received many awards, for example, the "neighbourhood's best cake shop" award! We also rarely receive unsatisfactory reviews. This is a short summary of our introduction of the shop to you.
