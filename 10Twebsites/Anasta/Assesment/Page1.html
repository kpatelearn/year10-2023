Introduction to Navarei


Navarei is a large and mountainous region situated near the South Western border of tbc. With its majestic Lake Seoro and famed Ovejarasa Peaks, Navarei attracts many tourists looking for a peaceful vacation. In summer, the average temperature is 20°C so it is not too hot. It is perfect weather for a hike up the mountains or a day of relaxation by the lake.
Meanwhile, in winter the temperature can go down to -5°C and when it is not raining, you can go skiing! It has a rural population of around 200 people living in villages in Navarei The people over there are really friendly and love to show people their culture and traditions.
