Lake Seoro


Lake Seoro is the biggest body of water in Navarei. It covers more than 1,289 kmÂ² and is 1,000 m above sea level. It is a great place to go swimming or kayaking in the summer and an even better place to ice skate in the winter. The average depth of the lake is around 180 ft, a good depth for scuba diving. There are also many marine life in Lake Seoro ranging from colourful fish to aquatic plants. All in all, it's a very good family place and a recommended vacation destination.
