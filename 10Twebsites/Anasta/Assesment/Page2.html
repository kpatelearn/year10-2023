The Ovejarasa Peaks


The Ovejarasa Peaks are a mountain range located in the region of Navarei. There are three mountains that make up this range; Lefenki, Enbija, Atsariv. Lefenki is the tallest mountain, have a height of 2,500 m. It is also an active volcano, with the previous eruption dating back to over 12,000 years ago. Nowadays, these three mountains are a great hiking spot for families because of their easy slopes and forest landscape.
