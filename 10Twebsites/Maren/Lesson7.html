HER INTERNATIONAL BEST SELLING BUTTER CHICKEN RECIPE!

Link to recipe video:

Ingrediants:

  • 4 chicken thigh fillets, trimmed and cut into 3cm pieces
  • 1 brown onion, halved, sliced
  • 3cm piece fresh ginger, finely grated
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 400g can diced tomatoes
  • 400g butternut pumpkin, peeled, cut into 3cm pieces
  • 300g white potatoes, peeled, cut into 2cm pieces
  • 1/4 cup thickened cream
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tbsp roughly chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 sheets frozen puff pastry, partially thawed
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
