Welcome to Lollyland!

Lollyland is a small island located in the Amdromeda galaxy.

Lollyland was created in around 2260 as a result of a catastrophic volcanic eruption. The volcanic eruption caused a unique mixture of gases, dust, and rock to fly into the atmosphere and stay there for longer than a century, which caused a significant change in its chemical composition. Later, the impact of a meteorite passing through the atmosphere at such high speeds caused the ixture of gas, dust, and rock in the atmosphere to fall from the sky in blasts of colour. After the gas, dust, and rock stopped falling, it created a beautiful island with candy shaped structures, now known as lolliland.

Lollyland not only boasts stunning scenery, but it is also home to the national animal of Lollyland: the lolmon! Lolmon live in ice cream houses that are dotted along the beach. Unfortunately, since the ice cream houses were formed as a result of the volcanic eruption, they are made of gas, dust, and rock and are not edible.
