Exports

...
Lollyland's main export is sugar. What makes lolmon sugar incredibly popular is that the taste is open to interpretation and tastes different depending on who eats it. Those whose favourite food is chocolate claim that lolmon sugar tastes like chocolate and those whose favourite food is dumplings claim that lolmon sugar tastes like dumplings.

Climate

...
As a result of its unique beginnings, lollyland's climate varies a lot. There are days where it is so hot that the ice cream houses that lolmon live in start to melt, and there are days so cold that the entire ocean freezes up.
