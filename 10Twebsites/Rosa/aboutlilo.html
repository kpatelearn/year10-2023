Lilo



Lilo is a large island located in Oceania in the South Pacific Ocean. Lilo is one of the 32 islands in the country Lani






The people of Lani speak the Language of Tantala, so If you want to greet someone in Tantalan just say "Maltah" or Tahta.
To say please you say "mali" and to say Thank you, say "Malo"





The picture on the left below is locted in Lilo. It is called the Aloha cave beach.
It's vibrant pink flowers on the top of the cave/mountain/rock is a very important and rare flower to the people of Lani, as it only blooms every 3 years.
The women of Lani go to this place while they are preganant, and pick 9 of the best looking flowers ontop of the rock/cave/mountain and preserve it til once the baby is born. Once the baby is ready to drink they will squeze all the juice out of the flower and mix it with milk to feed their baby with. Once they do this, them and their baby will be granted with a long and happy life.

on a full moon and make a wish at exactly 12pm your wish will come true.
