Riding whales at sunset

Come along and enjoy a once in a life time oppurtunity riding whales! As you ride on your whales back you can enjoy an incrediable view of the best sunsets you will ever see. We have different sessions depending on how long you want to go for: 30 minutes, 1 hour or 2 hours

Dinosaur Zoo

Feeling bored? Come have a blast at dinosaur zoo! A place safe to get close to the biggest animal on earth, you'll see many different dinosaurs in their habitats and if your lucky you get to see some of our friendly ones roaming around. This is perfect for people of all ages to visit!

Candy Mansion

If your a sweet tooth be sure to stop and look around at our Candy Mansion! Yes you heard me candy that means a mansion made out of candy. This mansion can house up to 500 people! The best candy in the world lives right here, you can even try the house itself. Whats taste testing wine when you can taste test candy, we are looking for the best candy eaters to come and join!

Magical forest

There's strolls in the park and then there's strolling though a magical forest. Let our tour guides take you on a enchanting experience through the magical forest! There's lakes that whisper, winds telling secrets and many more intriguing things, if you listen closley you can hear the voices of faeries in the sound of bells and if you see one we beleive you will have luck for the rest of your life. Make sure you pack your walking shoes and let this tour show you the beauty of our forest.
